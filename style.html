Colors & Typography

Color Palette

Manly Black, Clean White, Steel Grey, and Toolshed Gold – these are the primary Toolshed colors. Equivalent in nature, there is no hierachy here. What matters is which colors are best suited to the message you want to convey.

Toolshed Gold may be supplemented with lighter and darker versions. Secondary colors should be used sparingly and on minor details.

Grey tones can be applied to support the color palette.
