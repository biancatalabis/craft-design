Button Links

Buttons are important tools in showing a user's choice of options, therefore a button hierarchy exists. Buttons are all the same size, but hieracrhy is displayed through color choice and fill.

Primary Buttons are solid filled and should be used on hero components, landing pages, and as call to action buttons.

Normal buttons are solid silver only and should be used on products pages.

Tertiary buttons are transparent and black or white only. They should be used on actions that lower in importance than the primary call to action.
